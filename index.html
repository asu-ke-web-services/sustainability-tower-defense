Welcome!

Welcome to Sustain It, you are the Sustainability Ranger in the city of Tempe. The mayor has given you a task to help filter the city's water from the contamination coming from the ocean.
With the help of modern technology, you have access to the most advanced robots and utilities to clean our water system.
NOTE: You can access this screen at any time by clicking the level title "Cleaning Water" at the top of the screen.

Instructions

The mayor has given you a budget of 200 RP (Recycle Points) to start off. You can earn more by successfuly filtering out the unwanted objects passing through the stream. AKA: Destroying them


Some hazardous objects/materials to be aware of:
  • Oil blobs - They will clog up our pipes
  • Trash(paper, can, bottles) - May cause viruses to spread.

    • How To Play

    Currently there are three main instruments you can use:
  • Pew Pew: Basic algae infused laser capable of disintegrating oil blobs and most trash materials.
  • Papa Pew:Advanced version of "Pew Pew" with increased ranged and power.
  • Blob Stopper: Advanced wind powered vacuum capable of slowing down incoming materials.

    • Starting Out:
    1. You can purchase robots by clicking the desired item in the item menu bar and placing them on the map.
    2. Begin the wave by clicking the PLAY buton.
    3. You may place more robots anytime during the game as long as you have sufficient RP.
    4. Press P to show Hints/Shortcut menu

    INFORMATION

    Sustain It is a game that utilizes the concept of sustainability into a Tower Defense style game.

    1. Earn RP (Recycle Points) by successfully destroying enemies with your defenses.

    2. You lose Health if an enemy successfully reaches the end of the path

    The game ends when:

    • You successfully destroyed all enemies
    • Your health reaches 0
